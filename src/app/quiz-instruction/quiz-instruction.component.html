Instructions

  • The Quiz is a multiple choise question type quiz you are given 4 options.
  • There is only one correct answer out of 4 given options.
  • There are total 10 questions in the each quiz.
  • For every question you are given 30 seconds to tell the correct answers.
  • You need 7 correct answers to pass.
  • If the timer runs out, unanswered questions will be marked incorrect.
